Why CEREBRO-X?

The Challenge: >98% of small molecules and 100% of large biologics fail to achieve therapeutic CNS concentrations. Traditional DDS development requires 3-5 years and $10-50M per candidate.

Our Solution: A fully automated six-tier hierarchical data resolution system combining ChEMBL/PubChem databases, RDKit computational chemistry, thermodynamic modeling, PBPK simulation, quantum-informed prediction, and ML-assisted estimation.

Output: Publication-ready reports (HTML dashboard, PDF, Excel) with full scientific provenance, tier-coded confidence scores, and researcher override capability.

About CEREBRO-X

CEREBRO-X is a research-grade computational pharmaceutical pipeline developed at Ain Shams University, Egypt. The platform addresses the critical bottleneck of blood-brain barrier penetration in CNS therapeutics through systematic, physics-informed computational analysis.
