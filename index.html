The $1.9B BBB Drug Delivery Problem

Over 98% of small molecules and ~100% of large biologics fail to cross the Blood-Brain Barrier (BBB). Researchers spend months and millions in wet-lab trial and error. CEREBRO-X eliminates the guesswork through purely physics-informed computational engineering.
