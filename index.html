Live Case Study: Donepezil vs Rivastigmine

CEREBRO-X successfully differentiates between APIs with the same indication based purely on their structural properties.

DrugPropertiesTop-1 DDS WinnerBBB ScoreKey Differentiator
Donepezil LogP = 4.36, MW = 379.49 Solid Lipid Nanoparticle (SLN) High High lipophilicity perfectly fits lipid-based matrices.
Rivastigmine LogP = 2.76, MW = 250.34 PLGA Nanoparticle High Moderate lipophilicity requires polymeric encapsulation for controlled release.
Insight: The platform correctly identifies that lipophilic Donepezil benefits from lipid-based carriers while Rivastigmine performs better with polymeric carriers. No more one-size-fits-all guesswork.
